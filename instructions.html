Instructions

To generate a palette, you can click " " to load your own data(Please format your data to the Data Format Requirements) or use our example data by click " ".
After loading data, result will automatically appears when ready. You can also click " Generate " to get a new palette.
There are two different mode to generate a new palette: High Efficiency Mode and High Quality Mode. The difference between these two modes is iteration number: High Quality Mode has more iterations than High Efficiency Mode and takes longer time.
Larger palettes will take longer time than smaller palettes to make.
